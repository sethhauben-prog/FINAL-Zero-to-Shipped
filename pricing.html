Common questions

Everything you need to know before signing up.

Do I need a credit card to sign up?
No. The Free plan requires nothing but an email address. You only need a card when you choose to upgrade to Creator.
Which projects are always free?
The first two projects in every track are free forever — including future tracks. For Web Apps that's Project 1 (Chat, Build & Ship) and Project 2 (Build and Deploy with Claude Code). When the AI Agents track launches, its first two projects will be free as well.
Can I cancel any time?
Yes. Cancel from your My Profile page at any time. Cancellation takes effect immediately and you won't be charged again. We don't offer partial-period refunds, but you'll keep access until the end of the billing cycle.
What happens to my progress if I cancel?
Your account and all progress data stay intact. You'll just lose access to the locked projects until you re-subscribe. Any projects you completed while on Creator remain in your Project Library.
Will the price ever increase?
We'll give at least 30 days' notice of any price change — and existing subscribers will be grandfathered at their current rate for the duration of their active subscription.
Are payments secure?
Yes. All payments are processed by Stripe — we never see or store your card details. Connections are encrypted end-to-end.
